Workday's Kathy Pham and Salesforce's Paula Goldman discuss building responsible AI through practical frameworks rather than theoretical discussions. Goldman describes how Salesforce's Office of Ethical and Humane Use embeds ethics into product design through principles like "mindful friction" and AI command centers that keep humans in control. The experts emphasize that AI's role should augment human capabilities rather than replace them, requiring human-centered design at every stage of development.
