Workday's acquisition of Sana represents a strategic move to create an AI-powered workplace experience that shifts enterprise AI toward agentic systems. Sana's CEO Joel Hellermark discusses how the combination will allow intuitive communication rather than manual navigation to handle complex tasks. The partnership aims to fundamentally transform how employees interact with enterprise HR and finance technology.
